Two Pointers Pattern - 
	Core Two Pointer Patterns -
		○ For reversing the array       -                      sorted not required.
		○ Checking palindrome -                               sorting not required
		○ For pair-sum/type problems       -            sorting required.
		○ Removing duplicates from a array -        sorting required
		○ Merging two sorted arrays -                     sorting required
		○ Partitioning the array - Move small values left, big right - Sorting not required
